Coaching That Meets You

You're not a project to be fixed. You're a person to be supported, seen, and heard—and that's exactly what I offer.

I'm not just a coach with credentials (though yes, I have those too: a background in psychology, certifications in nutrition, mindfulness, breathwork, and health coaching). I'm an intuitive guide, an empath, and a deep listener who knows how to tune into what you really need—sometimes before you even know how to say it.

This work isn't one-size-fits-all. With me, it's personal.

We start by focusing on the most important relationship in your life: the one you have with yourself. You will build confidence, emotional strength, and a sense of worth that isn't waiting on anything outside of you. I use all the tools I've studied—but only if they serve you.

I'm here to help you feel good now, not "when."

If you're curious, let's just talk. No pressure, no prep—just a free, honest, 30-minute conversation to see if we're a fit.

My Journey

A Work in Progress

I haven't always been kind—to others or to myself.
There were times I let my pain speak louder than my truth. I looked for love in the wrong places, chose relationships that confirmed my insecurities, and doubted my worth more often than I'd like to admit.

I felt anxious all the time. Tired, but wired. Like I was constantly feeling like I was giving to everyone else, but never fully connected to myself. My nervous system was on overdrive, but I didn't know I could live differently.

I didn't know it at the time, but I was trying to prove I was enough—by chasing approval, by overgiving, by surviving instead of living.

Then I started to tune in to take a look at myself and my needs. I began to give out of love and started filling my own cup first. I stopped waiting for the perfect moment, the perfect version of me, or some finish line to finally feel good. I began changing how I saw myself—and with that, how I saw the world. I discovered tools that helped me breathe, heal, nourish, and move through life differently.

I'm still growing. I'm still learning. I'm still human. But now I do it from a place of self-compassion, clarity, and joy in the now—not waiting until I "get there."

And that's what I offer you.
Not a program to fix you.
A supportive path that meets your needs and goals—and to feel good while you're walking it.
